Our pickleball team, Pickle is Life

Here's my awesome teammate, Kaylie!

Pickleball is a fun and easy sport to pick up, especially if you have prior experience with racket sports. I had only played pickleball twice before starting this team, but I grew up playing tennis, so I played pretty well if I do say so myself. Anyone who has played pickleball at the courts on campus knows how difficult it is to get an open court. Wait times are long and the lines of pickle players anxious to play get rowdy. Signing up for a pickleball team secures you and a friend with a reserved court for an hour. Plus, more pickle friends to practice with!
