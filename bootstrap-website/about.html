About

Here's how it all started...

In the fall of 2025, my friend Emmy, the redhead pictured above, asked me to join her friend's intramural soccer team. I hadn't played soccer since I was in middle school, but the team was co-ed, so they needed another girl in order to play. Eager to help out my friend, I quickly signed up and went to Dick's Sporting Goods to buy some cleats. I wasn't very good, but I was having fun. One day, I was making plans with my friends, Sam and Kedin, and I told them I was busy on Monday nights because I had soccer. They told me they had their own soccer team too, and urged me to join. Since the intramural sports fee at VT is a one-time payment of $25 for the year, I agreed. The fall semester ended, but I was hungry for more. Sam, Kedin, Emmy, and I were browsing the intramural website for more sports to indulge in, and we found wallyball - it's volleyball, but you can bounce the balls off the wall. It's played in a squash court. Next, the three of us, along with some other friend, started an indoor soccer team. Then, I joined pickleball doubles with my friend, Kaylie. I've loved all 5 of my teams - it may be the best $25 I've ever spent. I encourage all Hokies to explore intramural sports, even ones they've never played. I sure will continue to do so myself.
